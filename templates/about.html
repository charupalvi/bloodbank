About Us

Welcome to Shri Bhavani Blood Bank, where saving lives is our mission. Founded in 2024, we are dedicated to providing a safe, reliable blood supply for hospitals, clinics, and emergency response units across Thane, Palghar and Wada. Every day, our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that patients in need have access to life-saving blood products, delivered with the highest standards of quality and care.

At Shri Bhavani Blood Bank, we believe that every donation counts. From our network of volunteer donors to our team of trained medical staff, each member plays a crucial role in supporting our communities. We work closely with healthcare providers, community organizations, and local leaders to spread awareness and promote the importance of blood donation.

Our Commitment

  • Safety First: Each donation undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets the highest safety standards.
  • Accessible for All: We strive to make blood donation easy and convenient, with multiple locations and mobile blood drives to serve all communities.
  • Continuous Innovation: We employ advanced technology and follow best practices to improve blood collection, storage, and distribution.

Whether you're a donor, recipient, or supporter, you're a part of the Shri Bhavani Blood Bank family. Together, we can make a difference and ensure that no one faces a blood shortage when it matters most.

Thank you for joining us in this life-saving journey. Every drop counts!
